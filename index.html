Who are we?

Brown Beans Co. is a third-party distributor emerging in the Ottawa market. We source high-quality coffee beans from local coffee producers and distribute them to coffee shops, restaurants, and local customers. Our goal is to support the local coffee industry and provide their customers with the freshest and best-tasting coffee possible.
